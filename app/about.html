Briefly...

Aldo is an Experience Design Lead at BCGDV (Manhattan Beach, CA).

With over a decade of experience in product, communications, and service design environments, Aldo has helped organizations large and small craft experiences that transform their relationships with consumers. His foundation in branding, creative technology, and design helps him bridge and lead technical and creative teams, creating unique solutions for brands in both the digital and physical landscapes.

He has developed conversational interfaces and experiential activations for Samsung Global, wearable apps and systems platforms for Fossil Group, social platform experiences for Walmart, and a multitude of web experiences for many other brands such as Google, SoulCycle, Johnson & Johnson, Verizon, Michael Kors, Air Jordan, and California Lottery during his time at R/GA, AKQA, and Casanova//McCann.

Aldo applies principles of human-centered design, visual communication, interaction design, cognitive & behavioral psychology, and human-computer interaction to projects, resulting in work that resonates with users and aligns with client needs, brand, and vision.

In academia Aldo studied electronics engineering at Cal. Poly., mass communications & advertising art direction at CSU Fullerton, and experience design & creative technology at VCU Brandcenter.
